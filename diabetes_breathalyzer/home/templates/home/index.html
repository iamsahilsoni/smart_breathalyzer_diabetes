About Us

We are final year students of TIET, Patiala with aim of providing a PAIN FREE device to measure blood glucose. Our Smart Breathalyzer can measure blood glucose from exhaled breath. Acetone present in the exhaled breath is the basis of working of Smart Breathalyzer.

LET'S BE NICE TO DIABETICS, THEY DEAL WITH ENOUGH PAIN ALREADY.
